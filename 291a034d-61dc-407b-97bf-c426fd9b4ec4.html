Description

AXYOS is an experimental first person shooter being made for players by players! The game itself combines different gameplay parts of classic FPS and MOBA.

Gameplay

Increase your speed, health, stealth or shield to became stronger than others! You should complete the objectives and upgrade character during the game session to lead your team to win! Use Ingame shop to buy new weapons and items.

The story

Inspired by giants of gaming industry, we made a decision to create our own game, which would inherit some key aspects of first person shooter and MOBA genres but was built relying on opinions of common gamers. This is how AXYOS came to be. Together, we can make this game happen!

Warning

We were trying to fix all the bugs but you should expect that this game still isn't working perfectly. Notice that this gameplay mix may be uninteresting for players who like the classic first person shooters or MOBA.
